Welcome to Cross River state The peoples Paradise

Cross River is a state in South South Nigeria, bordering Cameroon to the east.
Its capital is Calabar and its name is derived from the Cross River, which passes through the state. English, French, Bekwarra, Ejagham and Efik are major languages of this state.
